## 4. 프롬프트 전략 (AI티 방지)

### 4.1 공통 모디파이어 (모든 이미지 적용)

```
STYLE & CAMERA:
- Shot on Canon EOS R5 or Sony A7IV, 35mm f/2.0 lens
- Natural daylight only, no artificial studio lighting
- Subtle film grain, realistic color science (not oversaturated)
- Moderate depth of field (f/2.8~f/5.6) — avoid exaggerated bokeh

AUTHENTICITY RULES:
- NOT a staged advertising photo — looks like a candid photo taken by an actual visitor
- NOT perfectly symmetric composition — slightly off-center, natural framing
- Slight imperfections allowed: wrinkled tent fabric, casual chair placement,
  a stray leaf on the deck, asymmetric grill smoke, natural shadows
- People in natural candid poses — looking at mountains, cooking, chatting with each other,
  NEVER posing for the camera, NEVER perfect smile at lens
- Warm realistic skin tones (not overly smoothed like AI skin)
- NO perfect grass, NO perfectly arranged gear, NO AI-generated symmetry artifacts

FORBIDDEN:
- NO hyperreal "Instagram-filter" look
- NO tilt-shift miniature effect
- NO extreme HDR / dramatic clouds
- NO floating/impossible perspectives
- NO duplicated or morphed faces/hands (classic AI artifacts)
```

### 4.2 사이트1 전용 모디파이어

```
SITE 1 — CLASSIC TP CANOPY BBQ ZONE:
- Concrete deck platform (flat, smooth, light gray)
- Classic TP square canopy tent with pyramid/triangular roof (4 sides converging upward)
- Canvas material in ivory/cream color, side curtains optional
- Essential: BBQ grill positioned in FRONT of the canopy
- Essential: Tarp/awning extending from the canopy OVER the BBQ grill (covered cooking area)
- Tent capacity: 1 tent = 1 team = 1 concrete deck
- Background: exposed concrete F&B building with glass walls (for site1 wide/building shots)
- Low concrete parapet walls bordering the deck area
```

### 4.3 사이트2 전용 모디파이어

```
SITE 2 — WOODEN DECK BELL TENT ZONE:
- Wooden deck platform (warm brown timber, visible grain)
- Bell tent (large circular canvas tent with central pole, conical roof)
  OR safari tent (box-shaped canvas tent)
- Canvas material in ivory/beige, camping family-friendly size
- Pine trees providing natural shade nearby
- Adjacent wooden decks EMPTY or distant (generous spacing — "너무 촘촘하지 않게")
- Near concrete container management building
```

### 4.4 사이트3 전용 모디파이어

```
SITE 3 — CRUSHED STONE SELF-SETUP ZONE:
- Ground: crushed gravel (small gray/white stones)
- Various personal dome/tunnel tents (different brands, colors, sizes — NOT uniform)
- Campers brought their own gear — tables, chairs, coolers look personal
- Wide spacing between tents — minimum 2 empty tent-widths between each
- No wooden decks, no concrete — pure gravel ground
- Wide slope area on the left/front of the campsite
```

## 5. 실행 계획 (How)

### 5.1 준비 단계
1. Desktop의 참조 사진을 프로젝트로 복사
   - `사이트1.jpg`, `사이트1ㅇ.jpg` → `images/origin/site1_ref_01.jpg`, `site1_ref_02.jpg`
   - `사이트2, 3.png` → `images/origin/site23_ref.png`
2. 출력 디렉토리 생성: `images/naver-place/`

### 5.2 스크립트 작성
- **파일:** `scripts/generate-naver-place-samples.mjs`
- **기반:** 기존 `generate-nanobanana2.mjs` 구조 재활용
- **모델:** `gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview` (나노바나나2)
- **프롬프트:** 위 섹션4의 공통+사이트별 모디파이어 조합

### 5.3 실행
- 10장 × (API 호출 ~30초 + 10초 Rate limit 대기) ≈ **약 7~8분**
- 백그라운드 실행 (사용자 대기 최소화)
- 실패 시 60초 대기 후 자동 재시도 (기존 스크립트 패턴 유지)

### 5.4 검증 단계 (Check)
1. 10장 모두 생성 성공 여부
2. 각 이미지가 해당 사이트 특성을 정확히 반영했는지
3. 사람 등장이 부자연스럽지 않은지 (AI 아티팩트 체크)
4. 네이버 플레이스 가이드라인 준수 (최소 해상도, 워터마크 없음 등)
